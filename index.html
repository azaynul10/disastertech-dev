About

From Student to Disaster Tech Innovator

Six months ago, I was just another undergraduate computer science student. Today, my disaster coordination system has helped coordinate emergency responses across 6 countries, with outcomes still being evaluated.

My journey into disaster technology began with a simple question: "How can we use cloud computing to save lives?" This led me to develop cutting-edge emergency response systems using AWS services, combining real-time data processing, machine learning, and global infrastructure to create solutions that matter.

This portfolio showcases not just my technical skills, but my commitment to using technology for humanitarian impact. Every line of code, every AWS service, every optimization - it's all in service of making the world more resilient to disasters.
